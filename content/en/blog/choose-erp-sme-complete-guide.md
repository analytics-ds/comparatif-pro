An ERP structures all SMB management: accounting, sales, inventory, production, HR. The choice commits the company for 7 to 10 years and shapes its ability to grow.

## Key takeaways

1. Four criteria dominate: functional coverage, total cost, compatibility, scalability.
2. Average ERP budget for an SMB ranges from 15,000 to 80,000 EUR for initial deployment.
3. Odoo, Sage, Dynamics 365 Business Central and SAP Business One lead the SMB market.
4. Average ROI for a well-implemented ERP materializes between 18 and 36 months.

## Selection criteria

Functional coverage must match business processes (trading, manufacturing, services). Integration with existing tools (CRM, CAD, e-commerce) avoids duplicate data entry. The economic model (perpetual license, SaaS subscription) directly impacts cash flow.

## FAQ

### How to choose an ERP for an SMB?

Choosing an ERP for an SMB relies on functional coverage, total cost of ownership, compatibility with existing tools and scalability. Plan 6 to 12 months for a complete deployment.

### What does an ERP cost for an SMB?

An SMB ERP costs between 15,000 and 80,000 EUR for initial deployment, plus 3,000 to 15,000 EUR annual maintenance. SaaS solutions start at 25 EUR per user per month.

### What are the best ERPs for SMBs in 2026?

The most adopted ERPs by SMBs in 2026 are Odoo, Sage 100, Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central and SAP Business One.
