Mastering inventory is a major profitability lever: according to Capgemini data, companies with a performant inventory management system reduce storage costs by 20 to 30% and stockouts by 30 to 50%. **Inventory management software** has become an essential tool category for retailers, e-commerce companies, distributors and industrials.

This comparison analyzes the seven most relevant solutions on the market in 2026 according to activity size and logistics complexity.

## Why a dedicated inventory system

Maintaining manual inventory management or using a spreadsheet exposes you to several documented operational risks: gap between physical and theoretical stock, undetected stockouts, unsold overstocks, fragile traceability in case of product recall.

A modern inventory steering platform brings three operational benefits:

- **Real-time tracking**: entries (supplier receptions), exits (sales, transfers), adjustments (breakage, theft, inventories). Instant update with each movement.
- **Reorder optimization**: alert thresholds per product, automatic calculation of economic order quantity, suggestions based on sales history.
- **Regulatory traceability**: tracking by lot or serial number for regulated sectors, complete history for audits and recalls.

Integration with operational tools is also key. For physical retail, coupling with [NF525-certified POS software](/en/blog/best-free-pos-software/) allows automatic stock decrementation on each sale. For service activity with parts storage, integration with [CMMS for industrial maintenance](/en/blog/cmms-maintenance-software-comparison/) smooths spare parts tracking.

## Erplain: the French reference for small businesses and distributors

Erplain, French editor created in 2014, has established itself as the most-used cloud inventory management solution by francophone small businesses and distributors. Its positioning combines inventory and commercial activity (quotes, invoices, customers) in a single interface.

Key features in 2026:

- Unlimited product catalog with photos, barcodes and variants.
- Real-time inventory tracking by warehouse and variant.
- Integrated compliant quotes and invoices (with automatic stock decrementation).
- Purchase module with supplier orders and reception tracking.
- Physical inventory with import/export.
- Native integrations with WooCommerce, Shopify, Prestashop for e-commerce.

Erplain pricing starts at 9.90 € per month (Solo formula, one user) and reaches 49.90 € per month for Premium formula (unlimited users, multi-warehouse).

## Zoho Inventory and Odoo: multi-country and modular alternatives

**Zoho Inventory** fits into the Zoho One ecosystem and offers an inventory management module with particularly extensive international coverage: multi-currency, multi-language, integrations with Amazon, eBay, Etsy, Shopify, Walmart.

**Odoo** offers an Inventory module within its modular ERP suite. The Community Edition is fully free in self-hosting. Cloud Enterprise versions add complementary modules for structures wanting a [complete ERP solution for SME](/en/blog/choosing-erp-sme-complete-guide/).

For industrial structures with complex production constraints, MoySys and Sage X3 remain more advanced references at higher budgets.

## Dolibarr and Sellsy: free open source and all-in-one

**Dolibarr** is the most-used open source ERP/CRM in the francophone world. Its inventory management module is fully free in self-hosting and covers a surprisingly broad functional scope: products, movements, multi-warehouse, suppliers, purchases, sales.

**Sellsy** combines CRM, invoicing, accounting and inventory management in a single platform designed for European small businesses and SMEs. Structures combining commercial and rental activity can also couple Sellsy with [dedicated property management software](/en/blog/best-property-management-software/) if they manage real estate in parallel.

## How to choose your inventory steering software

Five criteria orient the decision:

**1. Product reference volume and catalog complexity**
Fewer than 500 references without variants: all cited solutions suit. Several thousand references with variants: Erplain, Sellsy or Odoo offer the best depth.

**2. Mono-warehouse or multi-warehouse**
Single storage location: standard version suffices. Multiple warehouses or sales points: premium or Enterprise version.

**3. Sales channels (physical, e-commerce, marketplaces)**
Pure physical retail: Erplain or Sellsy. Single-channel e-commerce: Erplain or Zoho Inventory. Multi-channel with marketplaces: Zoho Inventory or Odoo.

**4. Required traceability level**
Standard: all solutions. Advanced (lot + expiration date): Sellsy, Odoo, MoySys. Serial numbers: Odoo, Sage X3.

**5. Integration with existing ecosystem**
French tool stack: favor Erplain or Sellsy. International stack: Zoho Inventory, Odoo, NetSuite. Open technical stack: Dolibarr or Odoo Community.
