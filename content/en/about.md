## Our mission

Comparatif-Pro is an independent editorial platform dedicated to decision-makers, buyers and technical managers in companies. Our mission is to produce rigorous, factual and independent comparisons to help professionals select the equipment, software, training and services best suited to their needs.

## Our approach

Every comparison published on Comparatif-Pro is based on three principles:

- **Editorial independence**: we receive no compensation from brands to influence our analyses. Our content is funded through transparent partnerships declared at the bottom of each article where applicable.
- **Rigorous methodology**: our comparisons rely on manufacturer datasheets, applicable standards (EN, ISO, CE), field feedback and public market data.
- **Technical translation**: we explain technical concepts (ergonomics, metrology, standards) with a precise professional vocabulary that remains accessible to non-specialist decision-makers.

## Our areas of expertise

The editorial team covers six main topics:

- Office furnishing and workspace design
- Industrial equipment and instrumentation
- Professional software (ERP, CAD, CMMS, B2B SaaS)
- Continuing education and technical certifications
- Professional tools and personal protective equipment (PPE)
- Business services (audit, consulting, maintenance, subcontracting)
