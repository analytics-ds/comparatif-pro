## Our mission

Comparatif-Pro is an independent editorial platform dedicated to decision-makers, buyers and technical managers in businesses. Our mission is to produce rigorous, factual and independent comparisons to help professionals select the equipment, software, training and services that fit their needs.

## Our approach

Every comparison published on Comparatif-Pro is built on three principles:

- **Editorial independence**: we do not receive compensation from brands to influence our analyses. Our content is funded through transparent partnerships disclosed at the bottom of articles when applicable.
- **Rigorous methodology**: our comparisons rely on manufacturer data sheets, applicable standards (EN, ISO, CE), field feedback and public market data.
- **Technical popularization**: we explain technical concepts (ergonomics, metrology, standards) with precise professional vocabulary accessible to non-specialist decision-makers.

## Our expertise

The editorial team covers six main topics:

- Office furnishing and workspace design
- Industrial equipment and instrumentation
- Professional software (ERP, CAD, CMMS, B2B SaaS)
- Continuous training and technical certifications
- Professional tools and personal protective equipment (PPE)
- Business services (audit, consulting, maintenance, subcontracting)
