## GDPR commitment

Comparatif-Pro is committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of visitors' personal data, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws.

## Data collected

Comparatif-Pro collects the following data:

- **Navigation data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, visit duration (via anonymized analytics tools).
- **Contact data**: first name, last name, email, message, only when you voluntarily reach out via the contact form or email.

No banking data is collected on this site.

## Processing purposes

Collected data is used to:

- Improve editorial quality and user experience
- Respond to contact requests
- Establish anonymized traffic statistics

## Retention period

- Navigation data: 13 months maximum
- Contact data: 3 years after the last exchange

## Cookies

Comparatif-Pro only uses technical cookies required for site operation and anonymized audience cookies. No advertising or third-party tracking cookie is placed without explicit consent.

## Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict, object to and port your data. To exercise these rights, contact:

**contact@comparatif-pro.fr**

You can also file a complaint with the French data protection authority (CNIL, https://www.cnil.fr) or your local supervisory authority.

## Security

Comparatif-Pro implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

## Changes

This policy may be updated. The date of the last update is indicated at the top of the page.
