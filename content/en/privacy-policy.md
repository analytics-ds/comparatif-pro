## GDPR commitment

Comparatif-Pro is committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of its visitors' personal data, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act.

## Data collected

The Comparatif-Pro website collects the following data:

- **Browsing data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, visit duration (via anonymized audience analysis tools).
- **Contact data**: first name, last name, email, message, only when you voluntarily contact us through the form or by email.

No banking data is collected on this site.

## Processing purposes

The data collected is used to:

- Improve the editorial quality and user experience of the site
- Respond to contact requests
- Produce anonymized visit statistics

## Retention period

- Browsing data: 13 months maximum
- Contact data: 3 years after the last exchange

## Cookies

Comparatif-Pro only uses technical cookies necessary for the site's operation and anonymized audience cookies. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are deposited without explicit consent.

## Your rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, delete, limit, object to and port your data. To exercise these rights, contact:

**contact@comparatif-pro.fr**

You may also file a complaint with the CNIL (https://www.cnil.fr).

## Security

Comparatif-Pro implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against any unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

## Changes

This policy may be updated. The last update date is indicated at the top of the page.
